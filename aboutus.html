STORY TIME

Mono Cafe has been started from 2019 with a rocky start. Learning the subject Entrepreneurship in college, Mono Cafe has been a subject that have been brought up by four of its creator. Starting as a fiction cafe, one of them have created an actual plan to establish the cafe. Hence, Mono Cafe was born in 2019 with the exact layout, menus and services that have been planned in their assignment. So, do you like coffee or perhaps a croissant? Everything is affordable and lovely as it sounds. Don't forget to visit Mono Cafe and spend your time here. Who knows this cafe is going to be one of your safe haven?

VISION

MISSION

Prepare a special recipe and create various choices of toppings for the pastries so that our customers have the chance to custom their own menu.
